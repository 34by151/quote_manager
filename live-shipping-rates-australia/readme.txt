== Third-Party Policy: ==

**Live Shipping Rates Australia for WooCommerce** does not collect or store any personal data from your website visitors. However, the plugin communicates with third-party shipping carriers (such as Australia Post, Aramex, AramexConnect, Direct Freight Express, and Sendle) to retrieve live shipping rates. These carriers may process shipping-related data such as:

Destination postal code
Package dimensions and weight

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies and terms of service of the respective shipping providers to understand how they handle data.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= Can I use this plugin outside of Australia?
This plugin is optimized for Australian carriers, but it can be used globally if the carriers support international shipping.

= Does the plugin support different WooCommerce store currencies?
Yes, the Live Shipping Rates Australia plugin is designed to work with the currency settings of your WooCommerce store. It retrieves shipping rates from carriers in their standard currency and automatically converts them to match your store's currency display. This ensures that your customers see shipping costs in the same currency as your product prices, providing a consistent shopping experience.

= Will more carriers be added in the future?
Yes! We are actively working on integrating more Australian and international shipping providers.

= Does this plugin support live tracking?
Currently, the plugin focuses on rate calculations. Live tracking may be added in future updates.
